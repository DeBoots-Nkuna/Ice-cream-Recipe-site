Ingredients:

  • ▸ 1 cup mango puree
  • ▸ 1 cup coconut milk
  • ▸ 1/2 cup sugar
  • ▸ 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • ▸ Pineapple chunks, passion fruit, and toasted coconut flakes for garnish


Instructions:

  • ▸ Mix mango puree, coconut milk, sugar, and cream in a bowl until smooth.
  • ▸ Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn until thickened.
  • ▸ Freeze for 3 hours. Serve in a coconut shell bowl, garnished with tropical fruits and toasted coconut flakes.
  • ▸ Freeze for 2 hours. Serve in cones or bowls, garnished with fresh berries and berry syrup.
