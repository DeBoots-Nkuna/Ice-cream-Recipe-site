Ingredients:

  • ▸ 2 cups mixed berries(strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • ▸ 1 cup heavy cream
  • ▸ 1 cup whole milk
  • ▸ 1/2 cup sugar
  • ▸ 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ▸ Fresh berries and berry syrup for garnish


Instructions:

  • ▸ Blend mixed berries to a smooth puree.
  • ▸ In a bowl, mix cream, milk, sugar, and vanilla. Add the berry puree.
  • ▸ Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  • ▸ Freeze for 2 hours. Serve in cones or bowls, garnished with fresh berries and berry syrup.
